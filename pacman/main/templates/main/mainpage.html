How to play?

There are 2 types of player in this game as it is in phenomenon game, Pacman.
Pacman
It has 3 levels. You start at level 1 and can eat only apples. If you manage to earn 21 points, congrulations! You made it to level 2! Now you'd like to try breaking some walls or tasting bananas. If you are dedicated enough and earn 250 points, then hooooray! You just conquered the world of Pacman. Enjoy beating ghosts.
Revenge is a dish best served cold.
Ghost
So you choose dark side, huh? Do you like risks? Fine, but be careful when you eat pacmans, cause they might be level 3, which means they eat you. Just a friendly reminder, if you are eaten, half of your points will melt away. What would life be like if there is no risk at all?
